The Pig gEVAL viewer uses data from a clone based tile path. This includes chromosomes 1 to 18, X and Y of the pig genome. Much of the sequencing strategies and efforts utilizes the integrated highly contiguous physical map of the pig genome (Humphray et al, Genome Biology 2007,8:R139)

A live status of the current tiling path can be found using the webtool Chromoview.

Manual annotations of the pig genome can be found in Vega
