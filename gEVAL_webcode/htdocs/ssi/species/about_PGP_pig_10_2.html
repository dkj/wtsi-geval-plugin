The Pig gEVAL_10_2 viewer represents the Sscrofa10.2 assembly released in late 2011. Built using a hybrid de novo assembly involving a clone by clone sequencing strategy suplemented with illumina whole genome shotgun reads, the draft genome comprises of 2.60Gb assigned to chromosomes with a further 212 Mb in unplaced scaffolds.



Further information regarding this assembly can be found in the publication Groenen MA et al. Nature 2012.

A live status of the current tiling path can be found using the webtool Chromoview.

Manual annotations of the pig genome can be found in Vega
