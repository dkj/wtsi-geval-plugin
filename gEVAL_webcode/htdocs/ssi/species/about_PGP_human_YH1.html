Assembly

The human YH1 gEVAL viewer uses the Yan Huang genome assembly produced by BGI-Shenzhen. Announced on October, 2007, this assembly represents the first diploid genome sequence of a Han Chinese, a representative of Asian population. The human YH1 gEVAL viewer consists of 185,086 top level components, with the max length of roughly 3.4 Mb.

More information can be found on the BGI Yan Huang page.
