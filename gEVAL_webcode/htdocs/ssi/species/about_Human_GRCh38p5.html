GRCh38.p5 is the fifth minor update to the Human reference genome assembly GRCh38, which was released by the GRC on December 24, 2013. This minor update adds 24 corrections (fix patches) and 38 additional alternate sequences (novel patches) to GRCh38, but does not change the coordinate system

The GRCh38.p5 (GCA_000001405.20) gEVAL browser is built on the GRCh38.p5 release

  • The GRCh38 primary assembly including unlocalised and unplaced regions
  • 261 alternate sequence regions
  • 24 fix patches
  • 38 novel patches
  • the mitochondrial sequence (J01415.2)
plus
  • the hs38d1 set of decoy sequences generated by Heng Li (GCA_000786075.2)
