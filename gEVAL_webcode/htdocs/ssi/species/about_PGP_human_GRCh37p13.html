GRCh37.p13 is the thirteenth minor update to the Human reference genome assembly GRCh37, which was initially released by the GRC in 2009. This minor update adds corrections (fix patches) and additional alternate sequences (novel patches) to GRCh37, but does not change the coordinate system

The GRCh37.p13 (GCA_000001405.14) gEVAL browser is built on

  • the GRCh37 primary assembly including unlocalised and unplaced regions
  • 9 alternate sequence regions
  • 131 fix patches
  • 73 novel patches
  • the mitochondrial sequence (J01415.2)
